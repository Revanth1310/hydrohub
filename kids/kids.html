Water Tips for Kids

  • Always turn off the tap while brushing your teeth.
  • Drink water regularly to stay healthy and strong!
  • Take shorter showers to save water.
  • Help your parents water the plants, but don’t waste water.
  • Make sure to use a bucket for washing your car instead of a hose.
  • Spread the word: Tell your friends why saving water is important!
